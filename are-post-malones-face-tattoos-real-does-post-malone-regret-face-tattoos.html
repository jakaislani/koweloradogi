Post Malone is an American rapper, singer-songwriter, and record producer who was born Austin Richard Post.

He is most recognized as one of the burgeoning young stars in the hip-hop industry for his first single “White Iverson,” which peaked at #14 on the Billboard Hot 100 list and was subsequently certified quadruple platinum by the RIAA.

Malone has collaborated with a plethora of artists since his initial smash track.

Post Malone announced in February 2017 that his album ‘Beerbongs & Bentleys’ will be released in December of that year. Malone released the first single from this album, “Rockstar,” in September.

The song debuted at number one on the Billboard Hot 100 and remained there for roughly eight weeks. The song’s official music video was released in November 2017.

His album ‘Beerbongs & Bentleys’ was published on April 27, 2018, and went double platinum in the United States, Canada, and New Zealand.

It also sold extremely well in other countries, including Mexico, Norway, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland.
