Jason Geier Obituary: On the evening of New Year’s Eve in 2018, Jason “Chainsaw” Geier of Fort Recovery, Ohio, was diagnosed with colon cancer that had progressed to his liver and bladder. He passed away. Over the course of that year, Geier received numerous tests and chemotherapy treatments.

The scans came back negative; therefore, in January 2020, he was able to stop receiving treatments. However, in October, it was found that the illness had spread to his liver, and he once again began taking chemotherapy.

Jason resides in Fort Recovery with his wife, Melissa and their four young boys.
