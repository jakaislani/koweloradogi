Nadeshot Early Life

The American former professional Call of Duty player, Nadeshot, born Matthew Haag on August 3, 1992, is currently the creator, co-owner, and CEO of 100 Thieves.

Haag graduated in 2010 from Palos Hills, Illinois’ Amos Alonzo Stagg High School.

Haag held a job at a McDonald’s before taking part in professional gaming.

Haag is a former captain of OpTic Gaming and a former professional Call of Duty player.
