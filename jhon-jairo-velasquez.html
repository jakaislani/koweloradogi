Biography

Jhon Jairo Velasquez was born in Yarumal, Colombia on April 15, 1962. Nicknamed “Popeye”, he was a medium level hitman infamous for his work done on behalf of the Medellin Cartel. He confessed to more than 250 killings and multiple kidnappings during his 23 year prison sentence in Colombia before his 2014 probationary release. He was loyal to the infamous drug kingpin and head of the Medellin cartel Pablo Escobar. He was first admitted to the Colombian National Army before leaving to enter the school of apprentices for the Colombian Navy.
