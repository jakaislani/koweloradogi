Rising Music Star, Family Dynamics, and Personal Life Updates

Sonnet Noel Whitaker is an American rising music star. She has released albums like Mischief Grace, and N.F.S. Sonnet is also an actress who has appeared in the 2004 movie First Daughter.

But, it's not her professional achievements that sprung her to fame. Sonnet has been in the limelight since her birth as she is the first daughter of well-known American actor Forest Whitaker and his late ex-wife Keisha Nash Whitaker.

Is Sonnet Noel Whitaker Married?

Sonnet Noel Whitaker is not reported to have been married to anyone. Moreover, the First Daughter actress Sonnet does not seem to be currently dating anyone.

Whitaker does not prefer to discuss her romantic life in public. In addition to this, her absence on social media platforms has made it hard to figure out Sonnet's relationship status. Talking of her parents' married life, it ended in 2018 after 22 years of marriage.

Sonnet Noel Whitaker Parents Divorce Reason

Forest Whitaker and Keisha Whitaker first laid their eyes on each other in 1994. As Sonnet Noel Whitaker's parents are from an acting background, they met on the set of the film Blown Away.

Sonnet's parents tied the knot in 1996 in Montego Bay, Jamaica. The pair remained married for 22 years until The Last King of Scotland actor filed for divorce in 2018. Why did Forest and Keisha divorce?

Well, Forest cited irreconcilable differences as the reason for their divorce. Sonnet's parents were still supportive of each other despite their separation.

Sonnet Noel Whitaker's Mother, Keisha Nash Whitaker, Died 

Unfortunately, Sonnet Noel Whitaker's mother is no longer alive. Keisha Nash Whitaker left the world on December 7, 2023. She died at the age of 51.

Keisha's death was confirmed by Sonnet's father Forest and her sister True Whitaker via Instagram. Godfather of Harlem actress True wrote;
