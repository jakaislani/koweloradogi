James Corden also known as James Kimberley Corden is an English actor, comedian, singer, writer, producer and television host. As of 2022, James Corden’s net worth is $70 million. He is popularly known for hosting the popular television talk show titled The Late Late Show with James Corden.

Apart from that, he is also known for co-writing and working in the popular television series titled Gavin & Stacey. He has also been featured in the popular song titled Shout, an unofficial anthem of the England football team in the 2010 FIFA World Cup.

James Corden Wiki/Biography

Born on 22 August 1978, James Corden’s age is 44 Years Old as of 2022. He was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from Hillingdon, United Kingdom. He holds a British-English by nationality and has his belief in the Christian religion.
