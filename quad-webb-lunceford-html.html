Quad Webb - Age, Family, Bio

Bravo reality personality on the breakout series Married To Medicine who is recognized for her marital tips, her quick and healthy meals for the busy wife, for being an expert on pet products and decorating on a budget.

Before Fame

She graduated from Tennessee State University with a BS in biology and a minor in chemistry.

Trivia

She has donated to the Canine Assistants Foundation.

Family Life

She married Dr. Gregory Lunceford in 2012. She was born and raised in Memphis, Tennessee.

Associated With

She stars on Married to Medicine alongside Toya Bush-Harris.
