Michelle Thomas (September 23, 1968 or 1969 – December 22 or 23, 1998) was an American actress. She was best known for her roles as Justine Phillips (girlfriend of Theo Huxtable) on the NBC sitcom The Cosby Show, and as Myra Monkhouse (girlfriend of Steve Urkel) on the ABC/CBS sitcom Family Matters. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
