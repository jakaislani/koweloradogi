Dwayne Johnson Faces Kidnapping Lawsuit from Former WWE Wrestler

At present, Dwayne Johnson is one of the most well-known celebrities in the world, with earnings that are perhaps even higher than the sky. The actor, who last appeared in Black Adam, is praised for his successful transition from wrestling to acting and is well-known for his eccentric acting style.

Even while his career is growing every day and failures don’t impact him the same way they do for performers in real life, a lawsuit most certainly will. A former WWE star is making waves today, accusing The Rock of hating a scheme to abduct her and her children.

You did really read correctly. For those who don’t know, Trenasha Biggers is a former TNA Impact and WWE developmental star who, in her prime, went under the moniker Rhaka Khan.

Khan has been embroiled in a contentious legal battle for the past several months. In October of last year, she decided to include not just The Rock but also an entire entourage of celebrities and even certain government organizations.

Rhaka Khan has filed lawsuits against Dwayne Johnson, The Mix, Maryse, and numerous other individuals for allegedly plotting against her, her children, and the ongoing criminal trials in Texas. If the most latest Giant Freakin Robot report is to be believed, Rhaka Khan has listed Dwayne Johnson, The Miz and his wife Maryse, The Bella Twins, former backstage agent Steve Keirn, and numerous other WWE celebrities in a large $3 billion lawsuit.

Furthermore, Trenasha Biggers has claimed that a large-scale conspiracy involving businesses, governments, and famous wrestlers is trying to sabotage her case for child custody and aggravated kidnapping.
