How old is Heidi Montag Today: Biography,Net Worth &amp; more

Heidi Montag is a prominent American reality television personality, model, singer and actress. She rose to prominence after being part of the cast in the MTV reality series The Hills in 2006. She is one of Hollywood’s finest and a household name when you talk about television personalities in the United States.

Who is Heidi Montag?

Heidi Blair Pratt also known as Heidi Montag was born in Crested Butte, Colorado, on September 15, 1986. Her parents separated when she was just a kid. After the divorce of her parents she was moved to the state of Colorado where she was raised. After high school, she moved to California where she furthered her studies. Her first breakthrough came when she was casted for the MTV reality shows ‘Laguna beach’: The Real Orange County. This was after she had become a friends with Lauren Conrad who at the time was the main cast of the same show in her university.

Heidi made appearances in four episodes of the second season. She had to forgo her university degree at some point to become an assistant at Bolthouse Productions. Heidi moved to Los Angeles in 2006 to star in the Laguna Beach spin-off series The Hills. The show was a hit, and the actress gained a lot of recognition from it. Heidi is equally passionate about music, which she has shown in her debut studio album, Superficial, released in 2010. Heidi became known after The Hills and she went on to appear in several albums, television series, and reality shows. She made an appearance in the reality television show Celebrity Big Brother with her husband. The couple’s performance received a ton of attention from fans, and hence they were quite popular during its run.

Quite recently in 2019, Heidi Montag has starred as the main cast in the new series The Hills: New Beginnings. She also appeared in the seventh season of the game show Celebrity Family Feud in 2020. In addition, she released her single, Glitter and Glory. Not many of Heidi Montag’s affairs grab the headlines. However, her first affair was reported in 2005 when she allegedly started dating her co-star Jordan Eubanks. They shared good chemistry, but the duo broke up in a year because of Jordan’s ill-temper. She is married to Spencer Pratt and together they have two child Gunner and Ryker.
