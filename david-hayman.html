Biography

He is best recognized for his roles as Pavel in the 2008 film The Boy in the Striped Pyjamas and as Malcolm in the 1986 film Sid and Nancy. He is also known for his recurring role as DCS Mike Walker in the TV drama Trial & Retribution. He worked with Helen Mirren and David Harewood on the 1993 feature film The Hawk. He studied at the Royal Scottish Academy of Music and Drama in Glasgow and started his career as an actor at the Citizens Theatre.
