Tyler Lockett Bio, Age, Parents, Wife, Children, Net Worth: Tyler Deron Lockett is an American football wide receiver for the Seattle Seahawks of the National Football League.

He was born on September 28, 1992, in Tulsa, Oklahoma. Tyler Lockett attended Barnard Elementary, Carver Middle School, and then Booker T. Washington High School in Tulsa.

He played college football at Kansas State and was drafted by the Seahawks in the third round of the 2015 National Football League (NFL) Draft.
