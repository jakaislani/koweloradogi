Kanye West Headlines Lollapalooza In Chile

Recommended Videos

Last night in Chile, Kanye West closed out the Lollapalooza concert by giving fans a solid set of some of his bigger hits. It looked like one hell of a show as West ran through songs like Through the Wire, Gold Digger, Run This Town, and Runaway, just to name a few. If you weren’t there to see it live, which I assume most of you weren’t, there are a couple videos out now that will give you a look at what you missed.
